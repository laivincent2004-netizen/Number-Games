# Number Games

A standalone, offline-friendly suite of six science-inspired number puzzles. Launch the single-page app in any modern browser to enjoy everything without logins or network access. Play sessions, best scores, and completion times are saved locally so you can pick up where you left off.

## Features
- **Elegant hub** with a 2×3 grid of entries beneath the Number Games title plus quick access to language, theme, and history controls.
- **24 Game** that supports both 10-within numbers and full A–K decks, complete with a card tableau, expression builder, undo, and timer.
- **Sudoku** generator offering four solvable difficulties (Easy, Normal, Hard, Inferno) with note mode, three hints, and time tracking.
- **2048** implementation with keyboard/touch controls, animated tile styling, live score, and a persistent best-score record.
- **Minesweeper** boards across three difficulty presets with first-click safety, pause/resume, and clear-time logging.
- **Crossmath** puzzles that always resolve with digits 1–9 exactly once, assisted by row/column equations, validation, and timers.
- **Fill Blocks** (nonogram) challenges in 10×10, 15×15, and 20×20 grids with dual marking modes, clue rendering, and completion checking.
- **Customization menu** for language (English/中文), science-themed background palettes (default beige + grey-blue accents), and persistent history records stored in `localStorage`.

## Getting started
Open `index.html` directly in your desktop or mobile browser—no build tools or servers required. The experience is optimized for touch and pointer input, and everything runs completely offline.
